Japanese Style Curry

Description

Japanese curry is different from Indian or Thai curries. It is more of a brown stew and it can be mild or spicy, depending on your tastes. The curry roux, from no heat to very spicy, can be bought at any international grocery store.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetsble oil
  • 2 pounds of beef chuck
  • 3 onions
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • water
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 3.5oz of Japanese curry roux

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add beef and saute until brown
  3. Add onions to pan
  4. Add ketchup and Worcestershire sauce, stir
  5. Add cayenne pepper
  6. Pur in water to cover by 1-2 inches
  7. Add carrots and bouillon
  8. Simmer for 30 minutes, skim fat as needed
  9. Add roux
  10. Simmer for about 30 minutes more until meat and vegetables are tender
